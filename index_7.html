BUILT DIFFERENT.

I'm Michael — a self-taught developer, maker, and creator based in Titusville, Florida. I didn't come up through bootcamps or computer science degrees. I learned by building, breaking things, and figuring it out.

Before code, I spent years as a tattoo artist — precision work, custom designs, one client at a time. That same attention to craft is what I bring to every script and every app I build now.

I build tools for real people — not tech bros. Simple, useful, honest software that actually solves problems. If it's not easy to use, it's not done yet.

Everything I make lives under the SouthDreams umbrella — two brands, one mission: build something that matters.
