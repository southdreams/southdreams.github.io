South Dreams
Branches With Purpose

South Dreams is the parent company and main brand. Every branch carries the same rule: build practical things with heart, grit, and enough edge to feel alive.

Soft Rebel is the care-and-memory branch. Dirty Road Creations is the tools and automation branch. Iron & Ink is the visual design and merch branch. The South Dreams tools and agent projects support all of it.

The mission is practical: help people organize their files, preserve memories, build small income streams, create real products, and take control of their digital life without handing everything to the cloud.

South Dreams is the root. The branches are how the work reaches people.

Dirty Road Creations

Python utilities, folder cleanup, photo sorting, automation scripts, and tools that turn messy digital chores into one clean button.

Iron & Ink

Outlaw visual work: logos, stickers, print-on-demand graphics, product mockups, and rough-edged creative assets with a real point of view.

Soft Rebel

Legacy, grief, story, and memory tools built with care: journals, timelines, family history, and autobiography ideas that stay human.

South Dreams Agent

A private local assistant lane for building software, websites, automation, digital products, repo repair, and business systems.

Strong backup supplier for apparel and higher-control mockups. Usually good when quality consistency matters more than the lowest base cost.

Good for posters, wall art, cards, and global local-production fulfillment. Fits Soft Rebel memory prints, quote posters, and family-history stationery.

Faire / Wholesale Later

Mockup Workflow

Fastest path: create product in Printify, export its mockups, polish hero/social images in Canva or Photopea, then use Smartmockups/Mockey for extra lifestyle shots.
